Cities are being urbanized to accomodate the growing urban population and this is leading to a unplanned growth of buildings and infrastructure which is negatively affecting the environment. Cambridge planning department is planning a major retrofit of the central square(Cambridge) area and propose to add residential units along with commercial to achieve a higher FAR than present. The project looks at a sustainable method of adding density to the area by using newly simulation tools such as UMI, DIVA etc that are capable of simulating multiple buildings on an urban scale for energy, daylight, outdoor comfort performance. Coupled with planning and massing design, a solution with higher FAR, better performance and better ROI was proposed.
