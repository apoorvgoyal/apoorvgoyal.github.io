Kargyak learning center is a small 100 sq. mts. school in the Zanskar valley in the Himalayas in India. At 4200 mt above the sea level, kargyak village sees extreme weather in the winters and kids needed a place to go to school near to the village. The school was planned and designed to use only passive methods and materials owing not just to build sustainabily but also owing to the 3 day horse trek to transport materials. Therefore all materials except windows and glass were sourced locally from the site and local people were involved in construction. The house now complete houses about 20-30 kids and also serves as a rest place owing to the comfortable temperatures throughout the year.
