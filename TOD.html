Analysis conducted for the Delhi Development Authority for objectives stated below as a part of the under development TOD policy for Delhi:


1. To ensure that all dwelling units get minimum 2-hour solar access in at least one habitable area (living room, bedroom or private open space) on the shortest winter day of Dec 21 (Winter Solstice). (According to the proposed TOD policy).

2. To calculate the annual energy consumption of the entire TOD proposal around the Karkardooma Metro Station.

3. To analyze the feasibility and benefits of inclusion of renewable energy in the proposal/policy.

4. To suggest changes in the massing/master-plan based on the tests conducted above.

5. To calculate the additional financial loading on the developer to follow ECBC norms in the buildings and include renewable energy such as photo voltaic systems (PV).
