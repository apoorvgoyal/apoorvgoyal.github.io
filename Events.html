Speaker Session Recap: Infiltration in Building Simulation

Sept 16, 2011

We had three great presentations dealing with real case studies of building infiltration, comparing infiltration through certain building materials with national standards, and infiltration modeling guidelines for Energy Plus. NGRID was gracious enough to be our hosts and provide refreshments. Although we started late, due to technical difficulties, we had a good audience at NGRID and over 40 listeners online. The speaker presentations are liked below, along with a recording of the three presentations.

The speakers were: Wei Lam from Wiss, Janney, Elstner Associates (WJE), Hannah Durschlag, a graduate student at MIT, and Dr. Krishnan Gowri from Pacific Northwest National Lab.
