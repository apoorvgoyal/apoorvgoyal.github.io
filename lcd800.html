Aqua-light is a modular unit which utilizes daylight to provide diffused sunlight inside buildings. The aqua-light unit is composed of reclaimed PETE bottles re-molded into a longer lasting and robust form. The aqua-light relies on water to enhance the light quality through refraction and diffusion.
